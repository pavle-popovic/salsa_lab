The Mambo Basic (On2)

Target: Master the timing of the break step on the 2 and 6.

In this lesson, we break down the fundamental rhythm of New York Style Salsa. Unlike linear Salsa On1, we break on the second beat of the measure.

  • 1: Step in place (Prep)
  • 2: Break Forward (Left foot) - The Accent
  • 3: Step in place
  • 4: Hold (Slow)
